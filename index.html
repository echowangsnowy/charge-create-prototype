Create Charge

Create a new charge and optionally submit a funding request.

What's improved in this prototype

  • 1 Guided stepper — breaks form into logical sections to reduce errors and cognitive load
  • 2 Amount mismatch detection — real-time comparison flags discrepancies before submission
  • 3 Drag-and-drop evidence upload — supports multiple files with preview
  • 4 Inline field guidance — tooltip hints on confusing fields (hover the ? icons)
  • 5 Review & confirm step — summary view before final submission prevents costly mistakes
  • 6 Visibility & notification toggles — clearer controls with descriptions instead of bare checkboxes
  • 7 Auto-populated due date — defaults to 30 days from notice date, editable
  • 8 Customer-specific categories — category & subcategory options auto-filter based on selected customer (e.g., FirstKey Homes sees only Dues and Fees & Fines)
  • 9 Auto-populated notes — notes field pre-fills with the required template format based on category/subcategory selection, operators edit from there
